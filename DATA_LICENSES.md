# Data and Source-Text Licensing

## Short Version

The MIT License covers project software. CC BY 4.0 covers original project
documentation where stated. Neither license grants rights to the third-party
corpora, editions, translations, or source texts used by the research project.

## Public Demonstration Data

Every passage or metadata record included in a public demonstration dataset
must carry, directly or through a stable source registry:

- creator and work;
- edition or version;
- source URL or persistent identifier;
- public-domain status or specific license;
- required attribution;
- modification, transcription, or translation status;
- a note identifying any AI-generated translation or summary.

A passage may enter the public demonstration dataset only after its rights are
confirmed at the edition or version level. The age of the underlying ancient
or medieval work does not by itself establish that a modern edition,
translation, transcription, or database record is reusable.

## Local Research Library

The complete local research library and derived databases are not distributed
with this repository. They remain subject to their providers' and editions'
terms, including source-specific restrictions and attribution requirements.
The project's code and documentation licenses do not supersede those terms.

See `docs/PUBLICATION_BOUNDARY.md` for the operational publication policy.
